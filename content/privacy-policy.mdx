1. Information Collection. zkSynth does not collect or store any personal information beyond what is necessary for the operation of the Protocol. This may include your wallet address, transaction history, and other information related to your use of the Protocol. zkSynth may also collect and store anonymous usage data for the purpose of improving the Protocol.
2. Information Use. zkSynth uses personal information solely for the purpose of operating the Protocol and improving the user experience. We do not share personal information with any third parties unless required by law. zkSynth may use anonymous usage data for research and analytical purposes.
3. Information Protection. zkSynth takes reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, we cannot guarantee that your personal information will be completely secure.
4. Data Retention. zkSynth retains personal information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or as required by law. After this period, personal information may be deleted or anonymized.
5. Third-Party Service Providers. zkSynth relies on third-party service providers, such as price feeds, to operate. These service providers may collect and use personal information in accordance with their own privacy policies. zkSynth is not responsible for the actions or policies of third-party service providers.
6. Children's Privacy. zkSynth does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 18. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under the age of 18, we will take reasonable steps to delete the information as soon as possible.
7. Changes to Privacy Policy. zkSynth reserves the right to modify or update this Privacy Policy at any time without prior notice. Your continued use of the zkSynth Protocol constitutes your acceptance of any such modifications or updates.
8. Governing Law and Jurisdiction. This Privacy Policy shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the jurisdiction in which zkSynth is domiciled. Any disputes arising out of or related to this Privacy Policy shall be resolved exclusively through arbitration in accordance with the rules of the jurisdiction in which zkSynth is domiciled.
